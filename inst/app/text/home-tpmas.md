Based upon detailed analysis, evidence and clinical engagement, 92 types of acute hospital activity have been identified as potentially mitigatable (i.e. could be reduced by deliberate action).
These are called **Types of Potentially Mitgatable Activity** (TPMAs).

Here is the number of TPMAs by activity type:

* Inpatients
  - Elective: **23**
  - Non-elective: **30**
  - Elective and non-elective: **7**
* Outpatients: **16**
* Accident and emergency: **16**

Note that:

* some of the types of potentially mitigatable activity are **not mutually exclusive** and so they overlap
* care should be taken **not to simply add the TPMAs together** when considering the overall opportunity to reduce acute hospital activity
