The current evidence base means that the TPMAs are broad in definition.
Whilst the activity shown in the tool has been classified as 'potentially mitigable', no one would suggest that all such activity can or should be mitigated.

A judgement is required of what proportion of the activity could be reduced.
It should be informed by the knowledge and experience of those involved, alongside available evidence.

The tool supports this judgement in two ways:

1. The information shown within this app will help local systems explore variation in hospital utilisation through comparison with statistical peers and the national average.
1. The results of an expert elicitation exercise are made available in the tool.
Subject matter experts predicted how much activity could be mitigated nationally by 2039/40.
The results provide a useful 'outside' view but should not be interpreted as an expectation of what can be achieved locally.
