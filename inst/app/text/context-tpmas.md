Based upon detailed analysis, evidence and clinical engagement, [The Strategy Unit](https://www.strategyunitwm.nhs.uk/) have identified 92 types of acute hospital activity have been identified as potentially mitigatable (i.e. could be reduced by deliberate action).
These are called Types of Potentially Mitgatable Activity (TPMAs).

Some TPMAs focus on activity avoidance (i.e. avoiding inpatient admissions, or A&E attendances) and some focus on efficiencies (i.e. reducing inpatient length of stay).
