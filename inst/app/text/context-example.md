An example TPMA is 'Falls Related Admissions'.

This TPMA identifies emergency admissions related to falls in those aged 65 or over. Its focus is admission avoidance, and the tool allows users to explore variation in admission rates across local authorities or NHS trusts. You can read [a full description](https://connect.strategyunitwm.nhs.uk/nhp/project_information/modelling_methodology/activity_mitigators/inpatient_activity_mitigators.html#falls-related-admissions-ip-aa-016).

Note that some spells will fall into more than one TPMA. This TPMA overlaps with 'Older People with Frailty Admissions (High Frailty Risk)' and 'Emergency Readmissions Within 28 Days', among others.
